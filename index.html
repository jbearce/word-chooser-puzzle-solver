Word Dials Puzzle Solver

Suppose you have a puzzle where words have to be made. The word has a set length, and each character in the word is chosen from its own pile.

For example, let's say you have a 4-letter word.
Character 1 can be any of:
      A, C, R, G
Character 2 can be any of:
      N, F, C, L, E, J
Character 3 can be any of:
      O, D, N, E

This can form words "red", "and", "ale", and possibly more. Use the puzzle solver below to find all words that can be made from your puzzle.
